Few Words About Us

We are a team of TU Berlin students creating a plug 'n' play data minimizing toolkit for streaming pipelines.

Check out our toolchain components on GitHub:

Minimizer

A python based library which supports several de-identification concepts from simple noise perturbation to k-anonymity.

Kafka SPI

A wrapper around the Minimizer build for simple integration into Apache Kafka.

Kubernetes Helm Charts

Our Helm registry with charts to deploy the Kafka SPI on Kubernetes and setup an end-to-end Confluent based streaming pipeline.

Supported Platforms

We support the following platforms and mechanisms to donate your fitness data:

Strava

A running and cycling platform.
We support the donation of your activity data (OAuth).

Garmin

The activity insight platform of the sport wearables producer Garmin.
We support the donation of your activity data as well as daily health data (Basic Auth).

FIT File Upload

You are not using Strava or Garmin?
Don't worry, we support uploading of any .FIT fitness data files.
